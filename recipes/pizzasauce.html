Ever wanted to make your own Pizza sauce to impress not only yourself but your family and friends?

Well today we will be learning how to make this delightful sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ground oregano
  • 1 Â½ teaspoons dried minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients.

  2. Mix together tomato sauce and tomato paste in a medium bowl until smooth.

  3. Stir in oregano, garlic, and paprika to pizza sauce mixture.

  4. Enjoy!
